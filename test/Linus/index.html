About Me

I'm currently a team lead for BraveNewTalent where I spend most of my days writing PHP or CoffeeScript using libraries like Symfony2 and Doctrine2. My areas of expertise are software architecture, continuous integration, unit testing, design patterns, frameworks, Agile (mostly Scrum), TDD and leadership.

I have various open source projects on GitHub. The best example of my PHP coding style is my xFrame. It's an incredibly lightweight MVC framework and does some clever things to boot in less than 13ms on fairly average hardware. I've done various Android projects and a handful of Node.js / MongoDB projects, usually using the Express framework.

I'm a qualified Zend PHP5 Certified Engineer and experienced JavaScript, HTML, CSS and MySQL developer. I also like to play with include Node.js, CoffeeScript, Mongo, Java, Python and the occasional bit of Android development.

I spoke at the 2012 London PHP Conference on the unconference track about integrating Lean product development and Agile software development. This year I hope to speak about team building and software developer archetypes.

My real passion is working with a great team to deliver challenging products. I love reading, particularly about programming, learning, leadership and motivation.
