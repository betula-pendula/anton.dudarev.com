Our Practice

High Standards

A standard of excellence in personalized dental care enables us to provide the quality dental services our patients deserve. We provide comprehensive treatment planning and use preventive, periodontal, restorative and cosmetic dentistry to achieve your optimal dental health. Should a dental emergency occur, we make every effort to see and care for you as soon as possible.

Education & Prevention

As a practice, we are true believers that preventative care and education are the keys to optimal dental health. That’s why we focus on thorough exams – checking the overall health of your teeth and gums, performing oral cancer exams, and taking x-rays when necessary. We also know that routine cleanings, flossing, sealants, and fluoride are all helpful in preventing dental disease.

Uncompromising Safety

Infection control in our office is also very important to us. To protect our patients and ourselves, we strictly maintain sterilization and cross contamination processes using standards recommended by the Occupational Safety and Health Administration (OSHA), and the Canadian Dental Association (CDA).

  • 3D X-Rays

    For the safest and most accurate implant placement, a 3D x-ray is essential. That is why our office is equipped with a CBCT (Cone-beam computed tomogram) machine that assists us in placing your implants safely and with computer precision.

    Same-Day Crowns!

    Gone are the days when you would have to walk around for a whole week with a temporary restoration such as a crown or veneer. With our new technology, beautiful crowns, veneers and bridges are completed within a single clean, comfortable visit.

    Digital X-Rays

    We have had digital x-ray technology since 2006. Digital x-rays can reduce radiation exposure by up to 90% over conventional film and chemical systems, and as a bonus it's also environmentally friendly.

  • Laser-assisted Treatment and Surgery

    Our laser makes the treatment of cankers, cold sores and gum surgery virtually pain free. It also reduces the need for dental freezing and stitches, and promotes a much faster recovery.

    X-Ray-free Cavity Detection

    Do you have difficulty tolerating x-rays? Our cutting-edge, radiation-free detection methods can detect cavities before they become a problem. And by detecting cavities sooner, treatment is easy and painless.

    Relaxation Dentistry

    If you're nervous, we can help. We are a fully-accredited relaxation center and we can provide IV sedation, oral sedation or nitrous oxide (laughing gas) to make your appointment stress-free.
